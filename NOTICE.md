The diagram is a collage of cut-outs (cropped, background-removed) of third-party
images. Each one's source, author and licence is tracked in `points.json`
(`image_credit`, `image_license`, `image_source_url`) and listed in the
"Image credits" section of `essay.md`.

**Most images** are from Wikimedia Commons under CC BY, CC BY-SA, CC0, or in the
public domain. Reuse requires keeping the attribution; for CC BY-SA the
share-alike terms apply to the derivative.

replace those three with freely-licensed or original alternatives, or obtain
permission. The renderer makes this easy: drop a replacement into `images/` and
point that metaphor's `image_cutout` at it.
